Data Catalog

Every BEA input-output table processed by Wassily, in four machine-readable formats. Data are derived from the U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) Annual I-O Accounts, Summary Level (71-sector).

Provenance: Source data fetched from the BEA API (Annual Input-Output Accounts, Summary). Matrices A, A_square, L and derived series are computed from the Use and Supply tables using standard I-O methodology. See Methodology for full derivation details.

Matrices

Seven matrix types per year. Use — absorption matrix (industries × commodities); Supply — make matrix (industries × commodities); A — direct-requirements coefficient matrix; A_square — square (industry × industry) version of A; L — Leontief inverse (I − A)⁻¹; VA — value-added rows; FD — final demand columns.
