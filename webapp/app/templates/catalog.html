Data Catalog

Every BEA input-output table processed by Leontief, in three machine-readable formats. Data are derived from the U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) Annual I-O Accounts, Summary Level (71-sector).

{% include "_shared/provenance.html" %}
Educational / research use. The data on this site is reconstructed from BEA input-output accounts for research transparency and education. It may lag official BEA revisions or contain reconstruction error, and it is not a substitute for the original source. For authoritative figures, pull the canonical data directly from the U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis at bea.gov (Input-Output Accounts). We are not the original source.

Matrices

Seven matrix types per year. Use — absorption matrix (industries × commodities); Supply — make matrix (industries × commodities); A — direct-requirements coefficient matrix; A_square — square (industry × industry) version of A; L — Leontief inverse (I − A)⁻¹; VA — value-added rows; FD — final demand columns.
